    • Small mod for Sith Troopers Battle Pack

    27 September 2020

    I like the small sets as they are fun and they are over in a matter of hours.

    Sith Troopers looks cool (even though I don’t like the sequel movies) in their red uniforms but it would be great if the jet troopers looked like flying as shown on the box:

    So I started looking into giving them a flying effect.

    After a few experimentations this is what I came up with:

    I wished they made hinges in trans-clear as well which would make the effect more realistic but I think it’s still good. I didn’t know I had a red plate of that size so that was a happy coincidence as I think it matched the troopers’ armors.

    I added the build instructions (generated by Studio 2.0) to resources section.

    This is obviously a very simple build. But it was a fun exercise for me to dip my toes into the LEGO ocean.
